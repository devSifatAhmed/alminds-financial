overview

The Fedsec Group started its journey in August 1996 as a SEBI Registered Category I Merchant Banker.

The Fedsec Group offers bespoke merchant banking, capital market, valuation, trusteeship, tax, regulatory, transaction advisory and fund raising services to a substantial and diversified client base that includes corporations, institutions, individuals, family offices, private equity and public market funds.

Exploring Africa, is an initiative by Fedsec Group to help companies establish business presence in the growing African continent. Our services include assistance in obtaining necessary approvals, raising finance for Government and Private Players for undertaking infrastructure projects in Africa and assistance in market development.

We aim to be the most preferred platform trusted by entrepreneurs and business families for providing customised and fiduciary solutions through innovation and integrity.

We have a distinguished leadership team with a wealth of knowledge, a global network, key on-ground expertise and over 100 years of collective experience.

LEADERSHIP TEAM

Our team comprises of over 18+ professionals, with extensive experience across geographies and sectors including Pharma, Defence, Automobiles, Auto Ancillary, Agro & Chemicals, Energy & Resources, Education, Financial Services, IT & ITES, Manufacturing, Real Estate, Gaming & Entertainment, Life Sciences & Healthcare, Private Equity, Transport, Telecom & Media.
